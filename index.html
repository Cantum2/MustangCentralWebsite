What is Mustang Central?

Mustang central is the go to place for anything you need. They can help with every thing from financial aid all the way to housing. They are located just down the hall from the information desk.

What resources does Mustang Central provide?

Mustang central provides resources on financial aid such as scholarships and hathaway. They are also available to help you fill out your fafsa if needed!
